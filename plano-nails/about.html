Our story

A small salon
that never forgot your name.

Plano Nails has sat on E Park Blvd long enough that some of our clients first came in as teenagers and now bring their own daughters. We stayed small on purpose — so we could keep taking our time, keeping things spotless, and remembering how you like your nails.

01

How we do things

We’re a family salon, and it shows. You’re not a slot on a conveyor belt here — you’re the person in the chair, and we’re not rushing to the next one. If your cuticles need a little extra care or your polish isn’t quite even, we fix it before you leave.

Cleanliness is non-negotiable. Files and buffers come out of a fresh packet in front of you, metal tools are sterilized between every single client, and every station gets wiped down and reset. It’s the quiet stuff that keeps people coming back for decades.

And because we live and work in Plano too, our reputation is the whole business. That’s not a slogan — it’s twenty years of regulars who’d tell their neighbor exactly where to go.
