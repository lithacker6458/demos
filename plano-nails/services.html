Good to know

Asked and answered.

Do I need an appointment?
Walk-ins are always welcome. For busy times — Saturdays, evenings, before a holiday — a quick call to (972) 578-1300 saves you the wait.
How do you keep tools clean?
Files and buffers are single-use and opened in front of you. Metal tools are hospital-grade sterilized between every client. It’s the first thing our regulars mention.
How long does gel or dip last?
Most clients get three weeks or more before a fill. We’ll recommend the finish that fits your nails and your routine.
Can you take out work from another salon?
Absolutely. We do gentle soak-off removal and can start fresh — removal is free with any new service.
