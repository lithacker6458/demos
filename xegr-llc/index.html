Rebar Placement

Safety & Quality

Safety is the assembly.

A safe, efficient, and precise process isn't a policy line — it's how we place every bar and stress every cable. GCs judge a sub on safety before anything else, and we build to that standard on every job.

Safety program details / EMR available on request
  • Jobsite safety discipline. PPE, housekeeping, and hazard awareness held to the GC's site rules and our own — every crew, every shift.
  • Tie & placement inspection. Reinforcing checked against the drawings before it's covered — spacing, cover, laps, and supports verified prior to pour.
  • Spec-conformance QA. Placement built to the structural drawings and specifications, ready for third-party and GC inspection with no surprises.
