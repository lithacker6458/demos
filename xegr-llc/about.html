Home/About

Est. 2019 · Dallas / Mesquite, TX

A Full-Force Reinforcing Team.

XEGR LLC was founded in 2019 in the Dallas–Mesquite area on a simple idea: a reinforcing subcontractor should feel like part of the crew it works for. That's still how we run.

Our Mission

We integrate with your team.

Our mission is to seamlessly integrate with our clients' teams, understanding and embracing their unique culture, to deliver exceptional service that guarantees a safe, efficient, and precise assembly process for reinforced concrete systems.

For a subcontractor, that's the whole game. The best reinforcing crew isn't the one that shows up and does its own thing — it's the one that slots into your operation, works to your standard, and takes a problem off your plate instead of adding one.

How We Work

We welcome difficulty. We conquer deadlines.

Reinforcing is a demanding trade, and we built XEGR to be at its best when the job is hardest.

01

We welcome difficulty

Congested mats, tight tolerances, complex pours — the reinforcing other crews would rather not bid is the work we want. Difficulty is where a full-force team earns its keep.

02

We thrive in complexity

We improvise and adapt to our environment to overcome obstacles. When the site changes, the sequence shifts, or the schedule tightens, we adjust and keep the reinforcing moving.

03

We conquer deadlines

Reinforcing is on the critical path — it has to be done before the pour. We treat your deadline as ours, so the steel is ready when the concrete is scheduled.
