Common questions

Asked and answered.

Are estimates really free?
Yes — always, with no obligation. We walk the line, measure it, and put a number in writing. We'd rather earn the job with a fair price than trap you with a fee.
Which fence material should I pick?
Depends on the job: wood for privacy on a budget, vinyl for zero upkeep, chain link for dogs and utility runs, aluminum for looks and pools, board or wire for acreage. We'll price the options side by side so you can decide with real numbers.
Can you repair a section instead of replacing the whole fence?
Usually, yes. Leaning posts, storm-damaged panels, and dragging gates can often be fixed and matched to the existing fence. If a repair would just be throwing money at a fence that's done, we'll tell you that straight.
Do I need to be home while you build?
Not necessarily. Once we've walked the line together and agreed on the layout, plenty of customers hand us gate access and come home to a finished fence. Whatever you're comfortable with.
