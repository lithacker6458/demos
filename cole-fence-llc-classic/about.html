Our story

Fence people.
Not fence salesmen.

Cole Fence LLC is a local fencing outfit working out of 180 James Road in Roebuck. No call centers, no franchise playbook — just a crew that measures your line, quotes it in writing, and builds the fence like it's going up next to our own place.

01

How we work

We keep it simple. You call, we walk the property line with you, and you get a written quote that means what it says. No mystery line items, no “while we're here” upsells. If we hit rock, roots, or a surprise on the line mid-job, you hear about it before it costs you a dime.

A fence is one of the few things you buy that the whole street looks at every day. So we set posts deep, pull lines straight, and hang gates that latch clean — and we don't call a job finished until you've walked the line with us and said so.

And because we live here too, our reputation rides on every run of fence we leave behind. That's not a slogan — it's the whole business model. Wood, vinyl, chain link, aluminum, and farm fencing, built for Upstate weather and Upstate clay.
