Common questions

Asked & answered.

Do you take walk-ins, or should I book?
Both. Walk in any time during hours and we'll get you in a chair. If you'd rather lock in a time, call (972) 494-5997 or use the booking link and we'll hold your spot.
Is the straight-razor neck shave included?
Yes — a hot-towel straight-razor neck shave finishes every men's haircut. A full beard shave is a separate service on the menu above.
Do you cut women's and kids' hair too?
Absolutely. We're a barbershop and a full salon: women's cuts, color, highlights, perms, blowouts, and kids' cuts are all on the menu. One shop for the whole family.
How much is color or a perm?
It depends on your length and thickness, so we quote it at a free consultation before we start — no surprises. As a guide: gloss/toner and root color start around $75, all-over color runs $125–$175, and perms start at $100.
What are your hours?
Tuesday through Friday 10:00 am – 6:00 pm, Saturday 9:00 am – 5:00 pm. Closed Sunday and Monday. And when the sign says open, we're open — we don't close early on you.
