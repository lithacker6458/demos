Our Story

From one wooden
corner stand.

"Customers aren't just customers — you're friends."

Benny's started back in 1999 as a single one-flap wooden stand on a Texas corner. Benny, Shari and Sam ran it themselves, learned every customer's name, and always kept the prices honest.

Twenty-five years later, that little corner stand has grown into two full locations in Azle and Whitney — but nothing else has changed. Same family. Same handshake. Same promise: the most affordable fireworks in the Azle, Boyd and Springtown area, and personal service you just don't get at the big-box tents.

— Benny, Shari & Sam
