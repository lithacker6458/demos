Good to know

Asked and answered.

Do you take walk-ins or do I need an appointment?
Both. Walk-ins are always welcome, and we honor our posted hours — we don't close early on you. If you want a guaranteed time, book a chair ahead and we'll have it ready.
Are you just a men's barbershop?
No — we're a full barbershop and salon. Alongside men's cuts, fades, and straight-razor shaves, we do women's cuts and styling, color, highlights, perms, and kids' cuts. The whole family, one shop.
Who's going to cut my hair?
Often the owner himself. The Mendez family — Marcial “Duran” and son Abner — still work the chairs, backed by a small team of stylists. Four generations of barbers stand behind every cut.
Is the consultation really free?
Yes. Color, a perm, or extensions can go a lot of directions — so we sit down first, talk through the plan, and there's no charge and no obligation to book.

Let's get you in the chair.
