Good to know

Asked and answered.

Do you serve breakfast all day?
Breakfast tacos are on from the moment we open every morning. Call (469) 000-0000 if you want a big order ready for the crew.
Can I order for a party or event?
Absolutely. We do taco bars, trays, and by-the-dozen for birthdays, work lunches, and quinceañeras around Garland. Give us a day's notice and we'll set you up.
Are you on the delivery apps?
Yes — you can find us on the major delivery apps. But calling ahead for pickup is faster and keeps more of your money with us.
How hot are the salsas?
Roja is mild-medium, verde has a kick, and there's one behind the counter we only bring out for the brave. Just ask.
