Our promise

Always make room for a little beauty in your life.

Tucked into Fresno's Villaggio Shopping Center, Deluxe Nails and Spa is the calm, comfortable place to land after a long day. Soft music, gentle hands, and a color wall that goes on for days.

Guests notice the difference the moment they walk in: a clean, modern space with plenty of pedicure chairs, and technicians who take their time and ask your opinion before they start. It's why so many keep coming back, some driving in from out of town just for their favorite tech.

Hygiene is not an afterthought here. We use medical-level sterilization and fresh, disposable, one-time-use supplies for every single guest, standards we hold above the usual salon routine.
