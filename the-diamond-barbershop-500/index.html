The Shop

A real barbershop, run by real barbers.

The Diamond Barbershop is where Richardson comes to look sharp. Our chairs are run by some of the best barbers in the city, and we treat every cut like it matters, because it does.

Walk in and you feel it right away. Clean shop, good music, a barber who actually listens to what you want. Whether it is a skin fade, a scissor cut, a full beard design with a hot towel, or a first haircut for your kid, you leave looking like the best version of yourself.

Our barbers speak English and Español, so nothing gets lost in translation. Grab a coffee, water, or soda, relax in the chair, and let us handle the rest. That is why folks across Richardson and Dallas keep coming back, and why we have earned 4.8 stars across 295 Google reviews.
