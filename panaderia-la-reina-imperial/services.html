Common questions

Good to know.

How far ahead should I order a custom cake?
For most birthday cakes, 48 hours is plenty. For quinceañeras and weddings, reserve your date as early as you can — popular weekends book up, and earlier means more time to get the design exactly right.
Do you do a tasting for big cakes?
Yes — for quinceañera and wedding cakes we'll set up a flavor tasting so you're confident before the big day. Just call (972) 000-0000 to schedule.
What flavors and fillings can I choose?
Tres leches, chocolate, and vanilla are the favorites, with fresh-fruit and cream fillings available. Tell us what you're imagining and we'll tell you what's possible.
Can I order pan dulce for a big party or event?
Absolutely. We do trays of pan dulce, dessert boxes, and half-sheet cakes for parties, offices, and church events. Call ahead and it'll be boxed and ready for pickup.
