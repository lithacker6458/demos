Common questions

Asked and answered.

Do I need an appointment?
Nope. Roll up during business hours and we'll take a look. Calling ahead at (214) 892-9194 with your tire size just helps us have it ready when you get here.
Do you sell used tires, or only new?
Both. We carry new tires from the brands you know and quality used tires when you want to keep the cost down. We'll show you the tread on any used tire before it goes on.
How do I find my tire size?
It's printed on the sidewall of your current tire — something like 225/65R17. Read us those numbers and we can quote you over the phone.
Can you fix a flat instead of selling me a new tire?
If the puncture is in the repairable part of the tread and the tire's still got life in it, we'll patch it — that's usually the cheaper, honest call. If it isn't safe to repair, we'll tell you straight.
