How we work

We keep it simple. Tell us the size or the problem, we quote you an out-the-door price with mounting and balancing included, and that's the number you pay. No mystery shop fees. No “while we've got it up on the lift” upsells for parts you don't need.

If a used tire will get you safely down the road for a lot less, we'll say so. If a flat can be patched instead of replaced, we'll patch it. And if a tire really isn't safe to repair, we'll show you why before we recommend anything.

Because we're right here in Garland, our reputation rides on every set of tires we sell. That's not a slogan — it's why the same customers keep pulling back in off E Centerville Rd.
