Services

Everyday wellness.
And the day you hope never comes.

From your puppy's first shots to a senior cat's dental, Mercy Animal Clinic handles the full range of general veterinary care for dogs and cats. Not sure what your pet needs? Call (555) 555-5555 and describe what you're seeing — we'll point you the right way.

Wellness Exams & Senior Care

A calm, thorough nose-to-tail exam is the single best thing you can do for your pet. We check weight, teeth, heart, joints, and skin, talk through diet and behavior, and build a plan tailored to your pet's life stage.

  • ✓ Puppy, adult, and senior wellness plans
  • ✓ Early detection of dental, joint, and weight issues
  • ✓ Nutrition and behavior guidance, no judgment

Dental Care

  • ✓ Cleanings with pre-anesthetic bloodwork

Common questions

Asked and answered.

Can I book an appointment online?
Yes — on the live site you'll be able to request an appointment right from your phone, any hour of the day, and we confirm during clinic hours. Prefer to talk? Call (555) 555-5555 and a real person answers.
Are you taking new patients?
We are, and we'd love to meet your dog or cat. New pet parents can fill out a quick new-patient form ahead of time so the first visit goes smoothly. Bring any prior records if you have them.
How fast can my sick pet be seen?
We hold same-week slots for sick visits every day. Call us and describe the symptoms — we'll get you in as soon as we can, and tell you honestly if it's something an emergency hospital should see tonight.
Will I know the cost before treatment?
Always. For anything beyond a routine exam we give you a written estimate first, walk you through what's urgent versus optional, and let you decide. No surprise line items on the invoice.
Do you handle emergencies after hours?
We're a general-practice clinic with set hours, not a 24-hour ER. For true after-hours emergencies we'll direct you to a trusted local emergency hospital — and we'll be here to follow up the next day.
