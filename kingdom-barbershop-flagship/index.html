The Shop

A Chair Worth Coming Back To

Kingdom is an old-school barbershop built for the modern-day gentleman, right here in North Austin off I-35.

We keep it simple: listen closely, cut carefully, and send you out looking your best. Owner Victor is the name customers reach for again and again, praised for his thoroughness, attention to detail, and for actually hearing what you ask for. That same standard runs through every chair in the shop.

From a fresh skin fade to a traditional straight-razor shave, the work is grounded and dependable. And the door is open to everyone, in a warm and friendly atmosphere where you are welcomed like family.
