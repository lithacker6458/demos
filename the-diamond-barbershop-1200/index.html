Our Craft

Where Richardson comes to look its best

The Diamond Barbershop was built on one simple idea: a great haircut is worth doing right. Not rushed, not close enough, done right. Our barbers bring the precision of a modern shop and the patience of the old-school chair.

Walk in for a skin fade, a beard design with a hot towel and massage, or a straight razor shave, and you get the same thing every time: clean lines, honest work, and a barber who actually listens. English or Spanish, first-timer or regular, everyone leaves sharp.
