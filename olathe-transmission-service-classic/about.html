Our story

Independent. Local.
On your side of the estimate.

Around here, a shifting problem too often gets one answer from the big outfits: a full rebuild, quoted before anyone drove the car. We built this shop to be the other kind of transmission shop — the one that road-tests first, explains what it found, and quotes the fix you actually need.

01

How we work

It starts with a free diagnostic road test and a computer scan — not a quote. Transmissions are expensive enough that you deserve to know what's actually wrong before anyone talks money. When we do talk money, it's a written estimate: repair versus rebuild, side by side, in plain English.

If a $200 solenoid fixes what a chain shop would have rebuilt, that's what we tell you — even when it costs us the bigger ticket. And when a rebuild really is the right call, we do it the slow, correct way: full teardown, quality parts, a final road test, and a warranty in writing.

We live here too. Our reputation in Olathe rides on every car that leaves this shop, and that's not a slogan — it's the whole business model.
