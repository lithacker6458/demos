Common questions

Asked and answered.

Do I really need a rebuild?
Often, no. Solenoids, sensors, leaks, and low or burnt fluid can all imitate a failing transmission. The free road test and scan tell us which one you have — and we quote the least expensive fix that will actually last. A rebuild is our last answer, not our first.
How long does a rebuild take?
Most rebuilds are done within about a week of your approval, and the written estimate includes an honest timeline for your specific unit. If parts availability changes that, you hear it from us before it becomes your problem.
Do you work on my make and model?
Domestic, Asian, and European — automatics, manuals, CVTs, and 4WD/AWD drivelines. Call (555) 555-5555 with your year, make, and model and we'll confirm on the spot.
Is the work under warranty?
Yes. Rebuilds and major repairs come with a written parts-and-labor warranty, and the exact terms are printed on your estimate before we start — not discovered after.
