Common questions

Asked and answered.

Do you handle both cooling and heating?
Yes — it's in the name. A/C repair and service in the hot months, furnace and heating work when the cold snaps come through. One number covers the whole system, year round.
Do you work on new construction?
We do — complete system installations for new-construction projects are one of our core services. If you're building in or around Palestine, bring us in early and we'll size the system to the plans.
What's a filter & service contract?
A simple maintenance plan: we change filters and check the system on a schedule so you don't have to think about it. It's the difference between catching a weak part in April and losing cooling on a 102° afternoon.
Do you serve businesses too?
Yes — commercial servicing and installation are both part of what we do. Storefronts, offices, and shops around Palestine get the same straight pricing and dependable scheduling as our residential customers.
