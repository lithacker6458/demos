Common questions

Asked and answered.

How long does a full detail take?
Most interior details run a few hours; The Full Works is usually a drop-off-in-the-morning, pick-up-shiny-in-the-afternoon job. We'll give you an honest window with your quote — and we'd rather keep the car an extra hour than hand it back half-done.
Can you really get pet hair and smells out?
Yes — embedded pet hair, spilled milk, gym gear, all of it. Deep extraction plus targeted odor treatment handles the vast majority of cabins. If something truly won't lift, we tell you straight before charging you for it.
Is the quote really flat?
Flat means flat. We quote after you describe the vehicle and its condition, and that written number is what you pay. If we open a door and find something beyond the scope, you hear about it and approve it before we touch anything extra.
What if I'm not happy with the result?
Point at the spot. We re-do it — that's the satisfaction guarantee, and it's not fine print.
