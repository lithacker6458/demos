Our story

Small enough to care.
Picky enough to matter.

Mid-Town Auto Detailing started the way most good shops do: somebody in Killeen wanted their truck actually detailed — not run through a tunnel and sprayed with cherry scent — and nobody nearby would quote it straight. We decided to be the other kind of shop.

01

How we work

We keep it simple. You tell us the year, make, model, and how the car's been living. We quote one flat number in writing, and that number is the bill. No mystery add-ons at pickup. No “while we were in there” upsells. If we find something outside the scope, you hear about it before we touch it.

The work itself is done the slow, correct way: two-bucket hand washes instead of swirl-happy brushes, interior products matched to the surface instead of one bottle for everything, and the extra hour taken when a cabin needs it. Your seat rails get the same attention as your hood.

And because we live here too — same heat, same dust, same live-oak pollen — our reputation rides on every vehicle we hand back. That's not a slogan; it's the whole business model.
