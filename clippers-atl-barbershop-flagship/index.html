Clippers ATL is an upscale-casual barber lounge in Atlanta, led by Master Barber BJ. The reputation is built on two things at once: cuts that are genuinely precise, and a room that is genuinely welcoming.

BJ is known for crispy line ups and fades that hold, and he takes his time to get there. The vibe around the chair stays chill and clean, the kind of place regulars call cool and welcoming, where kids get nailed haircuts and every hair texture is at home.

Vintage-meets-modern decor, lounge seating, complimentary snacks and drinks, and parking on site. Not a chop-shop. A shop you plan your week around.
