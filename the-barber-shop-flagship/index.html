Our Story

An Established Member of the Neighborhood

Owner Gio Borroto has been in the hair styling industry for over ten years. After college he studied cosmetology and barbering in Philadelphia, then finished his continuing education in New York City at Devachan Salon, an authority in curly hair. He specialized in the classic dry cutting methods rooted in Sahag.

Gio believes in preserving the traditional methods and refuses to cut corners. He gets to know your hair's personality as well as your preferences, and builds the perfect cutting experience each and every time.

"We are looking forward to serving the community for years to come."
