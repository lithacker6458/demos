Common questions

Asked and answered.

Can I really buy just one bolt?
Yes. Most of our fasteners are sold loose — by the piece, by the handful, or by the box. Need exactly six stainless metric bolts? Buy exactly six.
I don’t know the size — can you still help?
That’s what the counter is for. Bring the broken part, the old fastener, or the thing it threads into, and we’ll match the diameter, pitch, and grade for you. Call (214) 555-0199 first if you want us to have it ready.
Do you carry metric and specialty sizes?
We do — metric threads, fine pitches, socket caps, set screws, U-bolts, and plenty of the sizes the big boxes dropped. If we don’t have it, we’ll tell you straight and often can point you to who does.
Can my business set up an account?
Absolutely. Trades, repair shops, and property managers run a tab with us for volume pricing, a will-call counter, and a single monthly invoice. Ask at the counter or through the contact page to get set up.
