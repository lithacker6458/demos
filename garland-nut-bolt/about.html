Our store

A whole store
for the part you need.

Garland Nut & Bolt is an independent fastener and hardware store on South Jupiter Road. We stock the nuts, bolts, screws, and specialty sizes that the big boxes stopped carrying — and we sell them the way a hardware store used to: by the piece, from someone who actually knows the difference.

01

How the counter works

Bring in the broken bolt, the stripped screw, or the mystery part off the machine. We’ll match the thread, the pitch, and the grade at the counter — SAE or metric, standard or specialty — and hand you exactly what you need. No wandering fourteen aisles hoping a blister pack has the right size.

Need one bolt? Buy one bolt. Need three hundred for a job? We’ll box them up. We sell fasteners loose, by weight, and by the box, so you pay for what the job takes and nothing you’ll toss in a drawer.

We’re a small, independent shop, which is exactly the point. The person who answers the phone is the same person who knows where the odd stuff lives. That’s the whole reason folks drive past the big box to get here.
