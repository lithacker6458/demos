Materials & finishes

Two materials.
Endless character.

Everything we make is steel and wood — but steel and wood are never just one thing. The finish is where your piece becomes yours. Here's the palette we build from.

01 / The steel

Cold-drawn,
hand-finished.

Structural on the inside, expressive on the surface. Every steel finish below is applied by hand and sealed to last in a working room. Hover a plate for its character note.

Blackened SteelDeep, near-matte black with real depth. Modern and architectural — our signature.Seen in: Rocket Mortgage desk
Raw Hot-RolledMill-scale character, sealed to hold its as-forged look. Honest and industrial.Seen in: North Face conference table
PatinaChemically aged greens and browns that read like reclaimed steel.Seen in: Watermark Church barn doors
Powder-CoatDurable, uniform color in any RAL. The contract-grade workhorse.Seen in: Solo Stove fixtures
BrushedDirectional grain with a soft sheen — refined without going glossy.Seen in: Tier One reception

02 / The timber

Real wood,
chosen board by board.

We hand-select every board so grain and tone carry through the piece. From bright modern oak to deep, figured walnut — pick the warmth your room needs.

White OakPale, clean grain that brightens a room. Our most-requested hardwood.Seen in: Athleta retail build
WalnutRich chocolate tones with dramatic figure. The luxury choice.Seen in: North Face boardroom
AshBold, open grain and pale color. Tough and expressive.Seen in: Scout & Cellar tasting bar
Reclaimed BarnwoodWeathered, storied timber — no two boards alike. Rustic soul.Seen in: Lane's Oak'd BBQ
Whitewashed OakOak with a soft, limed finish — coastal-warm and understated.Seen in: Trophy Companies office

Built to last

Sealed for real rooms

Every finish is chosen and sealed for the way the piece will actually be used — a lobby, a boardroom, a kitchen. It has to look right on day one and year ten.

Mix & match

Pair any steel with any wood

Blackened steel and white oak. Raw steel and walnut. Patina and barnwood. The combinations are yours — we'll help you land the one that fits the space.

Samples at your consultation

A screen only gets you so far. We bring physical finish and wood samples to every consultation so you can see and feel the real thing before you commit.
