The program

What a trade
account gets you.

Trade Pricing

A standing trade discount on every commission, scaled to your volume. The more you build with us, the better it gets.

Custom Fabrication

Send us your drawings, a napkin sketch, or a mood board. We shop-fabricate to your spec in steel and timber and detail it to match your design.

COM / COL Welcome

Have a customer's-own-material or customer's-own-leather spec? Ship it to us and we'll build around it — no fighting your vendor list.

Published Lead Times

Firm, honest timelines you can put in front of your client. Single pieces run 4–8 weeks; fit-outs are scheduled to your install date.

A Dedicated Contact

One person who knows your projects — quotes, drawings, samples, and status. No phone-tree, no starting over every time.

Wholesale & Volume

Outfitting multiple locations or reselling? We handle wholesale runs and multi-site rollouts with coordinated finishes and a single delivery plan.
