04 / Materials & finishes

Feel the finish
before you commit.

Steel and wood aren't one thing — they're dozens. Scroll the plates, hover for the character notes, and picture your piece. We'll bring physical samples to your consultation.

Full materials guide
Blackened Steel Deep, near-matte black with subtle depth. Our signature — modern, architectural. Ideal for: statement lobby & reception pieces
Raw Hot-Rolled Mill-scale character, sealed to hold its as-forged look. Honest and industrial. Ideal for: honest, industrial conference tables
Patina Chemically aged tones — greens and browns that read like reclaimed steel. Ideal for: reclaimed-look barn doors & feature walls
Powder-Coat Durable, uniform color in any RAL — the contract-grade workhorse finish. Ideal for: high-traffic contract & outdoor fixtures
Brushed Directional grain with a soft sheen — refined without going glossy. Ideal for: refined executive reception stations
White Oak Pale, clean grain that brightens a room. Our most-requested hardwood. Ideal for: bright, modern retail & office builds
Walnut Rich chocolate tones with dramatic figure. The luxury choice. Ideal for: warm executive offices & boardrooms
Ash Bold, open grain and pale color — tough and expressive. Ideal for: expressive bars & hospitality tops
Reclaimed Barnwood Weathered, storied timber — no two boards alike. Rustic soul. Ideal for: rustic restaurant & retail interiors
Whitewashed Oak Oak with a soft, limed finish — coastal-warm and understated. Ideal for: understated, coastal-warm offices

05 / How a commission works

Three steps,
zero surprises.

It's a conversation, not a checkout. Here's exactly what happens — and what it takes to start.

01

The Imagining

We talk through your space, your use, and your look — then sketch it and bring material samples. No obligation, no pressure.

What it costs to start: nothing — the consultation is free.

02

The Build

We finalize drawings, lock materials and finishes, and fabricate your piece by hand in our Texas shop. You get a firm timeline and a firm price.

Kickoff: 50% deposit to reserve the build slot.

03

The Delivery

We deliver and install — on time, on budget, cleaned up behind us. You sign off when it's exactly right.

Balance due on installation & sign-off.
