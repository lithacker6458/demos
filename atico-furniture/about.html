How a commission works

A conversation,
then a commission.

You never “add a reception desk to cart.” Great custom furniture starts with a talk about your space and ends with something installed and signed off. Here's exactly how we get there.

The commission, step by step

01

The Imagining

We start by listening. What's the space? Who uses it? What's the look you're chasing — rustic-industrial, clean modern, something in between? We walk your site or hop on a call, sketch options, and bring real steel and wood samples so you can see and feel the direction. You leave with a clear concept and a ballpark, not a bill.

What it costs to start: nothing — the consultation and estimate are free.

02

The Build

Once the concept is right, we finalize drawings, lock your materials and finishes, and hand-fabricate the piece in our Texas shop — cutting, welding, joining, sanding, and sealing. You get a firm price and a firm timeline up front, and we keep you posted as it comes together. No surprises, no scope creep.

Kickoff: a 50% deposit reserves your build slot; the balance is due at delivery.

03

The Delivery

We deliver and install ourselves — no drop-and-run. We place it, level it, and clean up behind us. You inspect the finished piece in its room, and you sign off only when it's exactly right. On time and on budget isn't a slogan here; it's the whole reputation.

Balance due on installation & your sign-off.

Before you ask

The questions
we hear most.

How much does a custom piece cost?
It depends on size, materials, and finish, but our Investment Guide shows honest starting points — accent tables from ~$600, reception desks from ~$4,000, conference and dining tables ~$3,000–$8,000, and full fit-outs quoted per project. Every quote is free and bespoke.
How long does a commission take?
Most single pieces run four to eight weeks from approved drawings to delivery; a reception desk is typically a six-week build. Full fit-outs are scheduled around your move-in date. We give you a firm timeline before you commit.
Do you do commercial fit-outs?
Yes — it's some of our favorite work. Offices, conference rooms, retail, restaurants, and churches. One point of contact, coordinated finishes across every piece, and a single install crew. Ask about our Trade & Designer program.
Do you deliver and install?
Always. We deliver from our Dallas / Farmers Branch and Houston shops, place and level the piece, and clean up. You sign off only when it's right.
Can I use my own material (COM/COL)?
For designers and trade clients, yes — we work with customer's-own-material and customer's-own-leather. See the Trade & Wholesale page for details.
Where are you located?
Two Texas shops: Dallas / Farmers Branch HQ at 3505 Garden Brook Dr, and Houston at 16830 Barker Springs Rd. Call (469) 620-2047 to set up a visit or consultation.
