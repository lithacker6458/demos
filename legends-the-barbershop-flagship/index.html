Our Story

Built by Goose, one handwritten card at a time

Legends began with Brian "Goose" Davis, a Youngstown, Ohio native who took an unlikely road to the chair. A UNLV poli-sci grad who came to LA for music management, Goose walked into a barbershop for a cut before a job interview. The owners befriended him, mentored him, and put eight hundred dollars into his first set of tools.

He enrolled at American Barber College under Mr. Gibson and was cutting full-time within six months. His edge was meticulous detail, plus handwritten thank-you and birthday cards to every client. It doubled his book in eighteen months.

"We came out of the gate as the standard bearer of excellence in the barber industry."

In 1999, with his mentors' blessing, he opened Legends. He remains the driving force and patriarch of the shop, his Youngstown roots on full display in his presence and his easy warmth. The mantra has never changed: everyone is welcome.
