Three levels of ceramic protection

A ceramic coating bonds to your clear coat and turns the surface glass-smooth: deeper gloss, easier washing, and years of protection from the Atlanta sun. Choose the depth of protection that fits how long you plan to keep the car.

01
3-Year Protection

Level One

A single-step polish to restore clarity, then a durable ceramic layer. The everyday choice for a lasting, low-maintenance shine.

02
5-Year Protection

Level Two

A single-step polish paired with a longer-life coating. More resilience against weather, wash marring, and time.

Lifetime
03
Lifetime Protection

Level Three

The highest level available. Maximum depth, maximum durability, backed for the life of your vehicle. Our flagship finish.

Paint Correction

One-Step Polish

A single machine-polishing stage that removes roughly 60% of the swirl marks and light imperfections dulling your paint, the fast route back to depth and clarity.

  • Removes ~60% of swirls and imperfections
  • Ideal prep before a ceramic coating
Paint Correction

Multi-Step Polish

A multi-stage correction for serious restoration, cutting through 80 to 95% of imperfections and swirl marks to bring tired or neglected paint back to a near-showroom finish.

  • Removes 80 to 95% of imperfections
  • Also handles overspray and scratch removal

Detailing, inside and out

From a quick refresh to a full interior-and-exterior transformation, every package is machine-applied and finished by hand. Sedan, SUV, and truck options for each.

Exterior · Level 1

Ceramic Wax Detail

A hand-finished exterior with a ceramic wax that beads water and holds its shine for three to four months.

  • Sedan, SUV, and XL options
Exterior · Level 2

Clay + Sealant Detail

Clay bar, iron remover, and a machine-applied sealant that decontaminates the surface and protects for six months or more.

  • Car, SUV, and large SUV / truck
Interior

Interior Detail

A deep clean of every touchpoint, from carpets and upholstery to the console and vents, so the cabin feels new again.

  • Sedan, SUV, and large SUV / truck
Combination

Mini & Full Detail

Two combined inside-and-out packages: a Mini Detail refresh, or a comprehensive Full Detail for a top-to-bottom reset.

  • Sized for cars, SUVs, and trucks
Upkeep

Maintenance Package

Steam cleaning plus hot-water extraction to keep a freshly detailed car looking its best between full services.

  • Sedan, SUV, and XL options

A craftsman's
whole career

For over 30 years, automotive care has been my passion and my livelihood.

It began in 1988, washing cars in my neighborhood, then weekends spent on boats at a local marina. In 1993 I gained invaluable experience at a high-end detail shop in Palm Beach, Florida, the work that set the course for everything since.

Six years ago I found my home at Atlanta Auto Spa, a cornerstone of Buckhead since 1996. As the owner and operator, I take pride in personally handling every vehicle that comes through the doors. Alongside my dedicated assistant, I put quality and your satisfaction above all else, guiding each client toward the service that truly fits, with honesty, transparency, and a commitment to integrity.
