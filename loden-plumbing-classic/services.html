Common questions

Asked and answered.

Are estimates really free?
Yes — always, with no obligation. We'd rather earn the job with a fair written number than trap you with a trip fee.
How fast can you get to me?
Most quotes happen within a couple of business days, and most jobs get scheduled the same week. If water is actively going where it shouldn't, say so when you call (555) 555-5555 — active leaks jump the line.
Do I need to be home while you work?
Not necessarily. Plenty of our Hopkinsville customers hand us access instructions and come home to a working drain. Whatever you're comfortable with.
What if the problem comes back?
Tell us. We come back and make it right — that's the satisfaction guarantee, and it's not fine print. A fix that doesn't hold isn't a fix.
