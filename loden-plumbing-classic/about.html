Loden Plumbing started the way most good trade businesses do: folks in Hopkinsville needed plumbing fixed right, and the big outfits kept treating them like a ticket number with a surprise invoice. We decided to be the other kind of company.

How we work

We keep it simple. You call, we listen, we look at the job with you, and you get a written quote that means what it says. No mystery line items. No “while we're in the wall” upsells. If we open a pipe and find something unexpected, you hear about it before it costs you a dime.

Every person we send to your door is someone we'd trust in our own home. We show up in the window we promised, we put down drop cloths and wear boot covers, and we don't call a job finished until you've run the water yourself and said so.

And because we live here too — same hard water, same aging pipes, same Christian County weather — our reputation rides on every single job. That's not a slogan; it's the whole business model.
