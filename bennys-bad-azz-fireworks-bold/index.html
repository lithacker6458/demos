The Family Behind the Boom

One Flap. One Family.

It started in 1999 as a one-flap wooden corner stand — Benny, Shari, and Sam serving neighbors out of a plywood booth. No frills, just fair prices and a handshake.

More than two decades later, that same family runs two locations across Azle and Whitney. What hasn't changed is the way we treat people. Around here, customers aren't just customers — you're friends. We'll help you build the right show for your budget, whether that's a single fountain or a truck bed full of cakes.

And because we mean it: military, police and firefighter discounts, every season, no questions asked.
