Our Story

From the Bay Area, with care

TB Nail Spa opened in November 2018 when owner Tammy relocated from the Bay Area to Northeast Fresno. She had already done nails for over thirteen years and run two salons before, and she brought all of it here.

"We care your health like we care our health."

That philosophy shows in every detail: sterilized tools, single-use kits, and unhurried craft, all so your nails last long and look beautiful. Alongside Tammy, nail-art specialist Hana brings the salon its signature hand-drawn and 3D designs.
