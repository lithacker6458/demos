Who We Are

"The best barbers of the city of Richardson and Dallas."

The Diamond Barbershop was built on a simple idea: a great haircut is more than a service, it is a ritual. From the moment you walk in you are handed a coffee, water, or soda, and from there the chair is yours.

Our barbers are bilingual and our doors are open to everyone, first-timers, regulars, dads bringing their kids for a first cut, and anyone who wants to leave looking and feeling their sharpest. Every cut ends with a crisp edge up and outline, because the details are what set a Diamond cut apart.

We earned 4.8 stars across nearly 300 reviews the honest way: professional skill, a clean and welcoming shop, and treating every client like a regular from day one.
