Our Craft

Where Richardson comes to look its best

We started The Diamond Barbershop to bring back the kind of haircut you remember: not rushed, not close enough, done right. Our barbers pair the precision of a modern shop with the patience of the old-school chair, and it shows the moment you sit down.

Whether you come in for a skin fade, a beard design with a hot towel and massage, or a straight razor shave, you get the same thing every time: clean lines, honest work, and a barber who actually listens. English or Spanish, first-timer or regular, everyone leaves sharp.
