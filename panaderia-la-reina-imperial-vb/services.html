Preguntas comunes

Asked and answered.

How far ahead should I order a custom cake?
At least 48 hours for most cakes, and a few extra days for large quinceañera cakes or detailed designs. The sooner you call (972) 424-9425, the better we can plan around your date.
What are your prices like?
Most items in the case run about $1–$10 — regulars tell us the prices are súper accesibles. Custom cakes are quoted by size and design when you order.
When is everything freshest?
We bake through the day, but mornings have the fullest case. If you want a specific item, calling ahead never hurts — popular pieces like conchas can sell out.
¿Hablan español?
Sí, claro. Somos una panadería familiar — llámanos al (972) 424-9425 y con gusto te ayudamos con tu pedido en español.
