Our Story

A nail appointment,
made social.

Mimosas Nail Bar is a Tran family owned salon in Charlotte. The idea was simple: turn a routine appointment into a treat you actually look forward to. The bar in Nail Bar is literal, with a mimosa in hand while your nails are made utterly fabulous.

The Metropolitan shop is the more central addition to the original NoDa flagship, opened in 2019, giving Midtown its own place to sit back, relax, and shine. Four ideas guide the room:

  • Simplicity is the essence of happiness.
  • Kindness in giving creates love.
  • Every great business is built on friendship.
  • Our best was good for today. Tomorrow we will do better.
