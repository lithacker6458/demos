Why folks keep coming back

Twenty years. Same family. Same promise.

Discount Fireworks of Texas has been family-owned and operated for more than 20 years, right here in Burleson. Two stands, two generations, and a whole lot of Fourth-of-Julys made brighter for our neighbors.

We're not a pop-up tent that vanishes on July 5th. We live here. When you pull up to the stand, you're talking to the family — and we'll happily walk you through what actually puts on a great show for your budget.

That's why we back it up with a 10% discount for military & first responders, and a Friends & Family referral program for the neighbors who send folks our way. Loyalty runs both directions around here.
