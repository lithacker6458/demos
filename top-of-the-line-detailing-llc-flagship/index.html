Our Story

From Porter To Owner, Built On Family

Owner Joseph Montoya Jr. was born and raised in Austin and grew up in the automotive industry, working his way up from porter to manager at a large Austin dealership and learning every aspect of automobiles along the way. In April 2014 he founded Top of the Line Detailing, bringing 25+ years of hands-on detailing experience.

"Owning my own company has always been a dream of mine, so I was able to combine the love of the auto industry and being my own boss."

Family is at the center of everything. Joseph has been married to his wife Gina for 19 years, and their two college-educated kids, John-Michael and Sylvie, are, in his words, the joys of their lives and the inspiration to work as hard as they do. He credits his honesty, professionalism, and humbleness, and says what drives him is the extreme satisfaction of his customers.
