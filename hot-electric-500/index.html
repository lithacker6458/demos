What We Do

Full-service electrical for homes & businesses

From a single flickering outlet to a full commercial panel, we handle it, cleanly and to code. Here is where most of our calls come from.

Panel Upgrades

Tripping breakers or an old fuse box? We replace and upgrade electrical panels to handle a modern home safely.

Wiring & Rewiring

New circuits, whole-home rewires, and troubleshooting dead outlets or shorts. We find the fault and fix the root cause.

Popular

EV Charger Install

Home charging done right. We install Level 2 EV chargers with the correct circuit and load calculation for your panel.

Lighting & Fixtures

Interior and exterior lighting, recessed cans, ceiling fans, and fixture swaps, installed level, safe, and looking sharp.

Outlets & Switches

Add, move, or replace outlets and switches, install GFCI and USB outlets, and fix anything that has stopped working.

New Construction & Remodel

Complete wiring for new builds, additions, and renovations, coordinated with your GC and inspected end to end.

Also handling surge protection, circuit-breaker repair, commercial service work and more. Not sure if it is us? Just call and ask.
