Good to know

Asked and answered.

Where exactly are you located?
We’re inside Plano Bazaar on Jupiter Rd in Plano, TX 75074. Look for us in the food court — call (469) 000-0000 if you need help finding the door.
Do you take call-ahead and to-go orders?
Absolutely. Call (469) 000-0000, place your order, and we’ll have it hot and ready for pickup. Dining in is always welcome too.
What are your most popular dishes?
The tacos al pastor and the quesabirria with consomé are what people come back for. On weekends, the menudo and pozole go fast.
Do you have vegetarian options?
Yes — bean and cheese tacos, quesadillas, elotes, and rajas can all be made meat-free. Just ask when you order.

Order’s waiting on you.

Call ahead and skip the wait. Your tacos will be ready.
