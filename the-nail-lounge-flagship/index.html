Our Story

A serene escape in The Shops at Boardwalk

The Nail Lounge is a locally family-owned establishment in the Northland of Kansas City. Our modern decor and serene atmosphere create the ideal setting to help you relax your mind and rejuvenate your body.

We hold the highest standards in sterilization and use no products containing MMA. Our technicians are skillfully trained and licensed, with monthly onsite educational classes, so the artistry keeps growing right alongside our color library.
