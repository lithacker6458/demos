Our story

Same chairs.
Same neighborhood. Since 2012.

Old East Dallas Barbershop is a family-owned shop that's been keeping the neighborhood sharp since 2012. No chains, no franchise playbook — just barbers who know your name, remember your cut, and take the time to get it right.

01

The shop

We opened our doors on Carroll Avenue in 2012 with a simple idea: a real neighborhood barbershop where you're a regular, not a number. Over a decade later, a lot of the folks in our chairs have been coming since day one — and now they bring their kids.

Fades, classic cuts, beard work, and the full hot-towel shave — our barbers do this all day, every day, and it shows in the work. We take the time to talk through what you want before we start, because a good cut begins with actually listening.

This is Old East Dallas, and this is our shop. We live here, our regulars live here, and our reputation rides on every single cut that walks out the door. That's exactly how we like it.
