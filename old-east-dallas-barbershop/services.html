Common questions

Good to know.

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?
Both work. Walk-ins are always welcome during shop hours, but calling ahead at (214) 560-7327 gets you a chair with less waiting — handy on Saturdays.
Do you cut kids' hair?
Absolutely. Kids 12 and under have their own friendly rate, and our barbers are patient with first-timers. Plenty of families make us their Saturday spot.
Cash or card?
Both. Tips are always appreciated and can go on the card too. Prices are confirmed in the chair before we start — no surprises at the counter.
Can I book with a specific barber?
Yes — just ask when you call and we'll get you in with the barber who knows your cut. Regulars are the heart of this shop.
