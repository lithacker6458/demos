the shop

A downtown Vegas classic, reborn.

Chops opened its doors in Downtown Las Vegas back in 2018 and quickly became the neighborhood shop, the kind of place where everybody gets treated like an old friend, no matter who you are.

After a full renovation, the owner relaunched the space as Chops 2.0. In his own words to the regulars:

"I know it didn't seem possible for our shop to get even better, but Chops 2.0 is insane. Come down and check out the new space. We are stoked to show it off."

Traditional straight razor craft meets modern fades and pompadours, with a vintage clothing shop in the back and a cold beer waiting up front. It reminds folks of the barbershop their grandfather used to go to, and that is exactly the point.
