Common questions

Asked and answered.

Do I need an appointment?
Not for everyday alterations — just walk in during shop hours (Tue–Sat, 10–4) and we'll fit you on the spot. For bridal and larger jobs, it's best to call ahead at (972) 234-8943 so we can set aside the time.
How long will my alterations take?
Most everyday hems and take-ins are ready in a few days. Suits and bridal take a little longer, especially in busy prom and wedding seasons — we'll give you a firm pickup date at your fitting.
How much does it cost?
It depends on the garment and the work, so we quote every piece up front at the fitting — no surprises when you pick up. Simple hems are our most affordable service; suits and bridal are priced by the job.
What if the fit still isn't right?
Try everything on before you leave the shop. If a hem or seam needs adjusting, we take care of it right there — a fit you're happy with is the whole point.
