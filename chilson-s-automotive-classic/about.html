How we work

We keep it simple. You tell us what the car is doing, we find out why, and you get a written estimate that means what it says. No mystery line items. No “while we're in there” upsells. If something unexpected turns up mid-job, you hear about it before it costs you a dime.

We'd rather show you a worn part than ask you to take our word for it. Plenty of what rolls through the door doesn't need the expensive fix — and when it doesn't, we say so. That costs us a few line items today and earns us a customer for years. We'll take that trade.

And because this is Lincoln, our reputation rides on every single car. Word gets around a town like this — that's not pressure, that's the business model.
