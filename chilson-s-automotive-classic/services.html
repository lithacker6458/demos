Common questions

Asked and answered.

Do you work on my make and model?
Almost certainly — we work on all common makes and models, domestic and import. If your vehicle is something unusual, call (402) 802-4923 and ask; you'll get a straight yes or no.
Are estimates really free?
Yes — written, itemized, and no obligation. We'd rather earn the job with a fair number than trap you with a fee.
Will you fix things I didn't ask about?
Never without asking. If we spot something while the car's on the lift, we tell you what it is, how urgent it is, and what it costs — and you decide. No “while we were in there” surprises on the invoice.
How fast can I get in?
Call and ask — most jobs get scheduled within the week, and if the car isn't safe to drive, say so and we'll do our best to shuffle things around.
