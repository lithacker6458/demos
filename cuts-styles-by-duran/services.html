Common questions

Asked and answered.

Do I need an appointment, or can I just walk in?
Walk right in — that's how most of Garland does it. If you'd rather not wait, call (972) 494-5997 ahead and we'll hold a chair for you.
How long is the wait on a Saturday?
Saturdays are our busiest, so there can be a short wait mid-morning. Come right at open (8am) or call ahead and we'll tell you the honest wait before you drive over.
Do you take kids and first haircuts?
Absolutely — kids 12 and under are $22, and first haircuts are one of our favorite things to do. We've got a booster board and plenty of patience.
Card or cash?
Both. We take cash and all major cards. Tips in either are always appreciated but never expected.

Come get lined up.

Walk in, or call ahead and skip the wait. We'll take care of you.
