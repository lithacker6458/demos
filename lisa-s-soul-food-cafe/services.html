Good to know

Asked and answered.

Can I call ahead for pickup?
Absolutely — that's the fastest way. Call (214) 372-3309, tell us your order and roughly when you're coming, and we'll have it hot and bagged.
Do you have daily specials?
We do. Certain plates like oxtails run on select days, and there's usually a special or two off the stove. Call to hear what's cooking today.
Can you cater my event?
Yes — repasts, church events, birthdays, and family gatherings are our thing. Give us a head count and a date and we'll put a spread together. A few days' notice helps for bigger parties.
Are there vegetarian options?
You can build a plate from our sides — mac & cheese, candied yams, green beans, and more. Note that some greens and beans are cooked with smoked meat; just ask and we'll point out what's meat-free.
