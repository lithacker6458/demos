Pho

Our reason for being. Beef bones and spices simmered for hours into a clear, aromatic broth, poured over rice noodles and your choice of cuts. The house special is a crowd favorite.

/02

Bún & Vermicelli

Cool rice vermicelli piled with grilled pork or lemongrass beef, fresh herbs, pickled veg, a crispy egg roll on top, and nuoc cham to pull it all together. Light but filling.

See bún bowls →
/03

Banh Mì

A crackly baguette stuffed with savory fillings, pate, pickled daikon and carrot, cucumber, cilantro, and jalapeño. The best sandwich you can grab on your way through Garland.

See sandwiches →
/04

Rice Plates · Cơm

Steamed jasmine rice with grilled pork chop, shredded pork skin, and a savory egg cake — the classic com tam plate — plus other rice combos that eat like a full meal.

See rice plates →
/05

Starters & Rolls

Fresh spring rolls, crispy egg rolls, and warm appetizers to open the table. Order a round for everyone while the pho comes up — they never last long.

Drinks

Vietnamese iced coffee with condensed milk, fresh lime and salted-plum sodas, hot tea, and sweet fruit smoothies. The right cold drink for a big warm bowl.
