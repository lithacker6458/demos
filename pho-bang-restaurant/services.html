Good to know

Asked and answered.

Do you have vegetarian options?
Yes — the veggie & tofu pho, tofu banh mi, and tofu vermicelli are all meat-free. Ask about swapping the fish sauce if you need it fully vegetarian.
Can I order takeout?
Absolutely. Call (972) 555-0100 with your order and we'll have it packed and ready. Pho broth and noodles pack separately so nothing gets soggy on the ride home.
How spicy is the food?
Our dishes come mild and let you build heat at the table with fresh jalapeño, sriracha, and chili oil. Tell your server if you'd like it turned up in the kitchen.
Do you take large or catering orders?
We're happy to help with bigger orders for the family or office — give us a call a little ahead of time so we can prep.
