The Salon

A serene sanctuary in Buckhead

Lavish Nail Bar opened in Buckhead in November 2022 and has grown into a genuine sanctuary for self-care in the Brookwood corridor. It is a large, open, light-filled space, eighteen pedicure chairs and twelve nail stations, so there is room to breathe and rarely a wait.

Our promise is simple: tranquil ambiance, a relaxing moment, and work you can trust. Bring us any photo and our techs will replicate it, quickly and without ever feeling rushed.
