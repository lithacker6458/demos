The Team

Rooted in craft. Designed for Uptown.

Uptown Cuts was founded in 2023 by Melanie Shope and Gwen Wilson with a clear idea: a barbershop where modern professionals can expect quality, consistency, and a little time to breathe.

Set inside the Hilton on South College Street, surrounded by the pulse of Uptown Charlotte, the shop serves people popping in between meetings or planning their monthly reset. Both owners cut hair, backed by a team that is skilled, friendly, and focused on detail, with 40+ years of combined experience.
