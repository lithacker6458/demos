Our story

Max's Mobile Auto Detailing started with a simple gripe: in Colorado Springs, CO your choices were a drive-through wash that scratches or losing your car to a shop for a whole day. We decided the detail shop should come to you instead.

How we work

We keep it simple. You tell us what you drive and what it needs, and you get a straight quote priced by vehicle size and condition. No mystery add-ons. No “while we're here” upsells. If we spot something worth flagging mid-detail, you hear about it before it costs you a dime.

Every detailer we send to your driveway is someone we'd hand our own keys to. We show up in the window we promised, protect your paint and your property while we work, and don't call a car finished until you've walked around it with us and said so.

And because we live here too, our reputation rides down every street we park on. That's not a slogan — it's the whole business model. Showroom shine, right in your driveway.
