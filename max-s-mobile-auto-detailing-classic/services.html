Common questions

Asked and answered.

Do I need to provide water or power?
No — the rig is fully self-contained. If your HOA or office park has rules about washing on site, tell us and we'll work around them.
How long does a detail take?
Interior or exterior packages usually run 1.5–2.5 hours; the Full Works runs 3–4 depending on size and condition. If it's urgent, say so when you call (555) 555-5555 and we'll do our best to shuffle the route.
Do I need to be there while you work?
Not necessarily. Plenty of our Colorado Springs, CO customers leave the car in the driveway or hand us keys at the front desk and come back to a finished detail. Whatever you're comfortable with.
What if I'm not happy with the result?
Tell us. We come back and make it right — that's the satisfaction guarantee, and it's not fine print.
