Our Promise

Built on a single word.

Trusted Nail Spa was built around one idea, and named for it. When new ownership took over the Tropicana flagship, formerly Crystal Beauty, they invested the time and money to upgrade and modernize the space from the ground up, so the name would actually mean something.

"We welcome you to discover and experience the essence of comfort and skill in our open and tranquil venue."

Our nail experts come from tight-knit neighborhoods around the city, part of a real Las Vegas tradition where nail art has been passed down through families for generations. The trust promise is concrete, not marketing: a 14-day guarantee, prices disclosed before we start, and strict sterilization on every visit.

If there is ever a problem, please contact us, and we'll make it right.
