Our Story

A Texas family that knows fireworks.

Over 30 years of retail experience, including 20 years in the fireworks industry, all under one family. We packed up from Porter in the Houston area and put down roots in Burleson, where our two stands at 13045 Rendon Road have served the Rendon community for more than 20 years.
