Straight answers

Questions we hear every week

Are you actually licensed and insured?

Yes. HOT Electric is a licensed and insured Texas electrical contractor, and the license behind the work is a Master Electrician license, the highest classification the state issues. Happy to show documentation before any job starts.

Do you pull permits and handle inspections?

Yes, on every job that requires them. We pull the permit, schedule the inspection and are on site when the inspector shows up. Unpermitted electrical work can void insurance claims and stall a home sale, so we never skip it.

Will I know the scope before work starts?

Always. After we diagnose the job you get a written scope describing exactly what will be done. Nothing beyond that scope happens without your say-so. It is how we work, on every job, for every customer.

Do you handle commercial properties?

Yes. Retail, offices, restaurants, shops and light industrial across Johnson County. We schedule around your business hours so the work does not cost you customers.

Can my panel handle an EV charger?

That is the first thing we check, with a real load calculation rather than a guess. Many homes can take a Level 2 charger as-is; some need a panel upgrade first. Either way you will know before you spend anything on hardware.

What counts as an electrical emergency?

A burning smell, a breaker that will not reset, sparking, a buzzing panel, or losing power to part of your home. Call 817-933-1981 any hour and we will talk you through making it safe. If you ever see flames or smoke, get out and call 911 first.
