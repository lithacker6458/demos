Panel upgrades and service changes

Add a workshop, a tankless heater or an EV and a tired 100 amp panel starts tripping. We replace it with a properly sized, properly labeled panel that gives you room to grow, permits pulled and inspection handled for you.

  • Load calculation before we recommend a size
  • Permits pulled and inspection handled for you
  • Every breaker labeled so you never guess again

Rewiring
04

Whole home rewiring

Brittle insulation, aluminum branch circuits and DIY splices hiding in the attic are how house fires start. We rewire occupied homes room by room, keep the mess contained, and leave you with copper runs that will outlast the roof.

  • Phased plans so you can stay in the house
  • GFCI and AFCI protection brought up to current code
  • Drywall cuts kept minimal and documented
Request a quote

Lighting design and installation

Good lighting changes how a room feels and how much it costs to run. We design and install recessed cans, under-cabinet runs, exterior security lighting and landscape lighting, planned before the first hole is cut.

  • Layout planned before any cutting begins
  • Dimmers and LED retrofits that do not flicker
  • Interior, exterior and landscape lighting

Ceiling Fans
06

Ceiling fan installs and replacements

A wobbling fan usually means the box was never rated for it. We install and replace ceiling fans with proper bracing and a fan-rated box, so they hang straight, run quiet and stay that way.

  • New installs and straight swaps
  • Fan-rated boxes and proper bracing
  • The wobble fixed right, not just tightened
Request a quote
EV Charging
07

EV charger installations

Charging overnight at home beats standing at a public charger every time. We install Level 2 chargers the right way: load calculation first, a dedicated circuit sized to the charger, and a clean mount where your cable actually reaches.

  • Panel capacity checked before you buy the charger
  • Dedicated circuit sized to the charger, to code
  • Garage, carport or outdoor rated installs
Request a quote

Troubleshooting and repair

A breaker that trips once a week is telling you something. Half-dead outlets, warm switch plates, lights that dim when the AC kicks on: we trace the actual cause with proper test equipment instead of parts-swapping and hoping.

  • Methodical circuit tracing, not guesswork
  • You see what we found before we fix it
  • Safety hazards flagged honestly, no scare tactics
