Our Salon

West Midtown's Kingdom of Nail Art.

Tucked inside the Interlock Tower, moments from Atlantic Station and Georgia Tech, Eleven NAIL Bar was built to be the ultimate destination for premium nail and beauty in West Midtown. Regulars describe a room that is genuinely relaxing, with skilled technicians who pay attention to every detail.

From a Classic Manicure to the La Vie En Rose Champagne pedicure, everything is done with a huge color selection, meticulous sanitation and a warm, unhurried welcome. Students get a discount, walk-ins are always welcome, and the wine bar is on us.
