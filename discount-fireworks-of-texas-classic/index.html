Our Story

Two decades of Texas fireworks, one family behind the counter.

Discount Fireworks of Texas is family-owned and operated — and has been for over 20 years. What started as a single roadside stand grew into two locations on Rendon Road, but the promise never changed: the best fireworks, honestly priced, sold by people who actually know the product.
