Meet the team

Genuinely affordable

Neighbors tell us our prices beat the clinics nearby — quality care shouldn’t mean sticker shock. We explain costs before we begin.

Deep local roots

Dr. Hamlin has practiced in the area since 1986 and opened Mercy in 2013. Decades of experience, right on Shiloh Road.

Rescue advocates

We work closely with DFW 501(c)(3) rescues and offer pre-adoption counseling — helping more pets find the right home.

Everything under one roof
