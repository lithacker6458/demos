## Removed / converted attributions (why)

- **"Daisy P. (Yelp Elite '26)" + "First to Review":** "Yelp Elite '26" and
  "First to Review" are invented credential/badge decorations, and the quote
  could not be confirmed verbatim against stored data. Converted to a clearly
  intentional placeholder card.
- **"Rustin Duza — Guest testimonial":** "Guest testimonial" is a vague,
  unverifiable source label, and the underlying quote was a first-party on-site
  testimonial, not a real Google/Yelp/Facebook review. Converted to an honest
  trust-signal card citing public, checkable facts (years in business, named
  technicians) instead of posing as a third-party review.
