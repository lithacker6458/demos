Our Spa

Hello beautiful, from the Heights

Ciao Bella Nail Spa sits in Houston's Heights, near Washington Ave and Memorial Park, in a beautiful new location that reviewers describe as spotless and peaceful. Though the name is new, the shop is well established, with roughly four-plus years of history and a loyal following carried right along with it.

"While there are many nail salons in the area, we stand out by providing thoughtful services where you'll be pampered to the fullest with the best."

Owned by Vi Francis Le, the spa leans into the good stuff: gold, collagen, milk & honey, and crystal jelly, all served with a complimentary drink in a linen-and-gold room. It is warm and inviting, never clinical.
