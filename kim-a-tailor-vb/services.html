FAQ

Good to know.

Do I need an appointment?
No — walk-ins are welcome during shop hours, Tuesday through Saturday, 10 am to 4 pm. If you’re on a tight deadline, a quick call to (972) 234-8943 first never hurts.
How fast can it be ready?
Fast is Kim’s reputation. Simple jobs can be done in as little as ~30 minutes, and plenty of work is same-day or next-day. Bridal and more involved pieces take longer — bring those in with time to spare.
Do you work on wedding gowns?
Yes — wedding gowns and bridesmaid dresses are part of what Kim does. For the best result, come in early rather than the week of the wedding, and plan for a fitting or two.
Eastern or Western styles?
Both. Kim alters Eastern and Western clothing alike — bring in whatever needs fitting and she’ll take it from there.
How much does it cost?
Pricing depends on the garment and the work involved, so Kim will quote you when she sees it. Regulars will tell you the value is in the quality and the speed — it’s worth doing once and doing right.
How do I pay?
Credit cards are accepted, along with the usual options. Just ask when you drop off or pick up.
