THE STUDIO

One chair. One tailor.
A whole lot of regulars.

Kim A Tailor is Ms. Kim — the owner who greets you, measures you, and does the work herself. For years, people across Richardson have brought her everything from work pants to wedding gowns, and kept coming back for the same reason: she’s genuinely good, genuinely kind, and genuinely fast.

01 / HOW SHE WORKS

The tailor is the shop.

There’s no counter of staff here and no ticket-number treatment. You explain how you want something to fit, Kim understands it quickly, pins it, and gives you honest advice about what will actually look best. That directness — and the smile that comes with it — is why people describe her as a pleasure to deal with.

The other half of her reputation is speed. Regulars talk about turnaround that’s “unbelievable”: rush jobs in half an hour, same-day and next-day work when they’re in a bind. When life needs something fixed right now, Kim is the name that gets passed around.

And it lasts. Some customers have trusted her with their clothes for more than ten years, with quality work every time and, as one put it, “never a complaint.” That kind of loyalty isn’t marketed — it’s earned, one garment at a time.
